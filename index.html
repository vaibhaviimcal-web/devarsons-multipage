Who We Are

Devarsons is an ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001 accredited company, developing and producing specialty colorants. The group was established in 1923 with manufacturing activity primarily based in Ahmedabad. With over 50 years of accumulated experience in Color Application, our product range is specifically designed to cater to customers in various industries such as Cosmetics, Crop Protection, Plastics, Petroleum, etc.

The emphasis within the organization is on developing new molecules or customizing current known molecules to meet highly specialized applications. Devarsons does not produce any one class of dye but rather an assortment of dyes with different chemistries to suit highly evolved applications. Our dyes comply with various worldwide regulations and our plant and product packaging are designed to eliminate foreign contamination.

Quality Policy

We, at Devarsons, are committed to and shall strive for continuous customer (both Internal & External) satisfaction by manufacturing consistent quality products through:

  • Continual improvement in products, process and system by adopting planning and proactive approach
  • Employee involvement
  • Satisfy applicable requirements

Our Quality Systems Include:

  • Quality manual, procedures, work instructions, specifications easily accessible by staff
  • Documented audit schedule, training records and certificates
  • Management of Change (MOC) program for process/materials/equipment
  • FMEA and probability/impact matrix for quality risk assessment
  • Full traceability of raw materials and batch records
  • Statistical Process Control (SPC) for quality critical parameters
  • Customer complaint investigation and trend analysis
  • Supplier management and approved supplier program
  • Housekeeping procedures to minimize contamination
  • Inventory management with stock rotation

Policy on Harassment

We are morally and legally committed to maintain the work culture of our organization free from religion, caste, regional, sexual, racial harassment or bullying.

  • Employees shall bring any and all such activities to notice of the management for appropriate necessary action
  • Employees can directly approach management without any fear and strict action would be initiated in time bound manner

Ethical Trading Policy

  • Risk management and compliance of Quality, Environment and Health Safety Standards
  • Fair business without paying bribes, kickbacks or giving anything of value to secure improper advantage
  • Encouragement of diverse workforce and provide a workplace free from discrimination, harassment or any other form of abuse
  • Treating employees fairly and honestly, including with respect to wages, working hours and benefits
  • Respecting human rights and prohibit all forms of forced or compulsory labour

Milestones

102 Years of Innovation and Excellence

1923

Foundation

Devarsons Group established as a trading company. Focus since inception was on being a "solution provider" rather than a dye supplier.

1960-70

Manufacturing Begins

Manufacturing established with Solvent Yellow 14 and Solvent Red 24. Targeted industries were Cosmetic and Petroleum.

1961

Company Incorporation

Established Devarsons Industries Private Limited

1976

Odhav Facility

Established Manufacturing facility in Odhav, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, INDIA

1978

First Export

First Export of Solvent Red 24 in container load to ICI Australia

1980

Range Expansion

Solvent Dye Range Expanded to include AQ series. Backward integrated to produce intermediates such as quinizarin. Established in-house DOS system.

1987

International Recognition

Established manufacturer of Solvent Dyes into COLOUR INDEX International, third edition. Published by THE SOCIETY OF DYERS AND COLOURISTS.

1990

Global Partnership

ICI UK (later Zeneca, Avecia) decided to source solvent dyes from Devarsons. Solvent dye range expanded. Liquid Solvent Dyes, Direct Dyes introduced.

1992

Innovation Milestone

Successfully developed Kerosene Marker Dye for Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL). First time launch "Blue Liquid Dye" for kerosene in India.

1995

DOS System

Established DOS system for full fledge use in centralizing company's database for routine tasks.

2003

FDA Approval

Successfully implemented, inspected and approved by Food Drugs Control Administration, Gujarat State

2005

ISO Certification

Successfully Implemented, inspected & approved by ISO 9001, ISO 14001

2009

Safety Standards

Successfully Implemented, inspected & approved by BS OHSAS 18001

2012

R&D Recognition

Set up DSIR recognized R&D and Pilot Plant. Established Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software for centralizing company's database information.

2013

Environmental Award

Government of Gujarat awarded "Gujarat Cleaner Production Award 2012-13"

2018

SAP Integration

Established SAP Business One software for streamlining end-to-end operations and boosting profitable growth with centralized information that allows real-time access to data across all departments.
